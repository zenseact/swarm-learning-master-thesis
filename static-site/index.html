Towards Federated Fleet Learning Using Unannotated Data

About the Project

This project is the outcome of our Master's thesis at Zenseact, where we delved into the emerging field of Federated Learning (FL) within the context of autonomous driving. The research primarily tackled the challenge of using scarce or non-existent labelled data, a major shift from the prevailing assumption of having copious amounts of labelled data.

Results

Our findings underscored the compelling potential of FL in autonomous driving, especially when the methods leverage on-vehicle generated labels or utilise semi-supervised or unsupervised learning approaches. Despite the complexity of the problem we addressed, our work provided valuable insights and contributed to a broader understanding of applying FL in the autonomous driving domain.
